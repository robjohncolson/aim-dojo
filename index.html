QUAKE III · RHYTHM TRAINER

AIM DOJO

A slow groove sets the pulse, with a woodblock tick on every beat to count by. An orb appears on a beat, somewhere around you — beat 1 you find it, beat 2 you track it, beat 3 you fire. Killing it within 3 beats is Excellent; faster is better still. The ring sweeping the floor marks each 8-beat phrase. Distance is in the sound: close orbs are dry and loud, far ones quiet and washed in reverb.

🎧 Headphones help, but laptop speakers work too. Sound on.
